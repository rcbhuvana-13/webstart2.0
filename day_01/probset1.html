HISTORY : Seshadripuram College's history began in 1930 when Smt. Anandamma and Smt. Seethamma, two educational enthusiasts, established a primary school with 20 students in a two-room building in the Seshadripuram area of Bangalore.

COURSES PROVIDED : Undergraduate- BCOM,BCA,BBA. Postgraduate- MCOM,MBA,MCA.
